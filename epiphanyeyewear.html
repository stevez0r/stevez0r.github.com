Stephen Zito

Epiphany Eyewear

UX & Product Design

Consumer targeted video sunglasses in the wearables sector, allowing users to stylishly and affordably record, share, and edit video through a mobile device and browser.

I collaborated with the CEO and 3D/Product Designer to create the branding for the product along with marketing and packaging materials. The product website we designed was essential in bringing in users from press articles and crowdfunding websites. The primary call-to-action was a buy modal that allowed users to easily choose the amount of storage they wanted in their glasses.
