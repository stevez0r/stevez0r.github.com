Badjrr

UX/UI Design

After winning a Tapjoy hackathon, Badjrr was incorporated as an internal team tool.

Badjrr boosts team morale and engagement by gamifying the workplace, allowing team members to easily create and award "badges" or achievements to co-workers for their accomplishments.
